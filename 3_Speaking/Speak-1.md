## Introduction
### 口语考试两大环节
+ 非计分环节：ID Checking四大问题
	+ Good morning. full name -> 不要太多拓展
	+ call you what -> shorter and friendlier **Leo**
	+ Where are from -> **I'm from Yun Meng , Hubei Province**
	+ Could I see your identification, please!-> **Ok. Here you are**
+ 计分环节：Part1、2&3
